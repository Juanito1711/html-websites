Key Details

The stadium was scheduled to host the finals in 2020 and 2021 but were both moved due to the COVID pandemic.

Who's winning the NBA Champions?

In Game 1 of the NBA Finals between the Denver Nuggets and the Miami Heat, Nikola Jokic led the Nuggets to a commanding 104-93 victory. Jokic, in his finals debut, recorded a triple-double with 27 points, 14 assists, and 10 rebounds. Jamal Murray contributed 26 points, while Aaron Gordon and Michael Porter Jr. added 16 and 14 points, respectively. The Nuggets showcased their depth and offensive firepower, overwhelming the Heat, who struggled with their shooting throughout the game. Bam Adebayo led the Heat with 26 points and 13 rebounds. Game 2 will take place in Denver, where the Nuggets will look to extend their series lead.
